Power Electronics

Power electronics is a specialised field of electrical engineering that deals with the conversion of electrical power. It is about how to convert electricity from one form to another. It is about how to convert the electrical power from the current generated by the power supply to the electrical power that is required by the loads. Power electronic devices are used in many different applications.

Power Electronics

They are used to power motors, lights, computers, and appliances.They are also used to control and protect electrical systems. One of the most common power electronic devices is the power inverter. They convert DC (direct current) to AC (alternating current) and vice versa. The inverter is usually connected to the mains and converts the incoming voltage to a different voltage.
